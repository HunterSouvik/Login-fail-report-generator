User Guide & Matching Logic

Step 1 & 2: Upload Reports

Upload your Fail Report and Success Report (.xlsx or .csv). The tool will automatically detect the column headers.

Step 3: Upload Template

Upload the Excel Template you want to use. The tool will look for "LOGIN-Failure" and "LOGIN-Success" tables within the template.

Step 4: MAC Matching Logic

Select the MAC Address column for both reports. The tool uses a "Fail-First" logic:

  • LOGIN-Failure: All records from your Fail report are included. Map your "Reason" or "Status" column to see why logins failed (e.g., Wrong Room).
  • LOGIN-Success: Only success records that have a matching MAC in the Fail report are included.

Final Generation

The output will be a single Excel file with professional zebra-striping, borders, auto-filters, and total record counts.
